ABOUT PACE

PACE: P. N. Institute Admission cum Entrance Test, which is one of the biggest scholarship cum admission tests in the state that proposes students studying in Class 5 to 11 with a chance to earn upto 100% scholarship and cash awards to procure the right path contributing to their GOALS.

With PACE, we aspire to look for and foster the students possessing genius and help them to work towards their ambition. Any students from class 5th to 11th (Opting for Science or Commerce) of English medium can register for PACE.

The scholarship amount is compensated in the student's fees.

PACE is taken in the form of Computer Based Test (CBT) only at the P. N. Institute’s Manjalpur branch.

A student can receive up to a 100% scholarship.

Students can appear for PACE during the scheduled exam dates announced by the institute.
