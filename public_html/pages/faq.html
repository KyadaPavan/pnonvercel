FAQs

Call us for any information. Mail us for any query. Visit us to chechout our wonderous premises and to get enrolled.

What is the admission procedure?

For those seeking admissions, an informal interaction will be conducted in which pupil and both the parents have to be present. The final decision of the admission committee will be binding on PACE.

What is the fee structure?

Please contact the institute for details.

What curriculum will the institute follow?

P. N. Institute follows GSEB/CBSE curriculum.

How many terms will the institute have?

We will be having two/three terms as per GSEB/CBSE.

What will be the institute timings?

Institute timings are: 7:45 am to 3:00 pm.

Will the institute authorities be taking adequate measures to ensure hygiene?

P. N. Institute maintains a high standard of cleanliness and hygiene. There are regular checks by the administration and coordinator.

What is the kind of security offered to students?

CCTV in all classrooms. Students are always accompanied by teachers or staff when moving around.

How often will there be PTMs?

After each term assessment.

Other than PTMs, when can a parent interact with teachers?

Parents can meet teachers with prior appointment anytime if needed.

How many students will be there per division?

We maintain 30 students per class for effective learning.

Do you have career counselling?

Yes, we have a dedicated Career Counselling Cell (CCC) for student guidance.

Are you participating in competitions?

Yes, for results and details, please visit the “RESULT” section of our website.

How do you support students weak in a subject?

We arrange remedial classes and provide feedback to parents for close monitoring.
