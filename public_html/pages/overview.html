About Us

A Place To Grow,
Learn & Play

At P. N. Institute, education goes beyond textbooks-it’s about nurturing the whole individual. We empower students in Science and Commerce to develop a lifelong love for learning, encouraging them to think critically, explore new ideas, and build strong moral values. Guided by the principles of dignity, fairness,bs truthfulness, and responsibility, we create an environment that embraces individuality.

Our approach blends academic excellence with personal growth, ensuring that each student is not only prepared for academic success but also for the challenges of life. From well-equipped laboratories to modern teaching methods, we provide the tools and support students need to thrive.

At P. N. Institute, we believe in linking learning to life, preparing students to excel in whatever path they choose.

    • PNF has slum school for children who can’t afford school. It takes a big heart to shape little minds. PNF preaches education, Values, beliefs & habits to slum kids of Vadodara on every Sunday.
